Coldplay: Music of the Spheres World Tour

Saturday, December 2, 2026
07:30 PM (GMT+7)
Gelora Bung Karno Stadium, Jakarta, Indonesia

About This Event

Join Coldplay for their spectacular Music of the Spheres World Tour. Experience an unforgettable night of music, lights, and incredible performances as they bring their latest album to life along with all their greatest hits.

With stunning stage production, mesmerizing lighting, and a promise of sustainability (this tour has been designed to have a significantly reduced environmental impact), Coldplay will deliver a concert experience you'll never forget.
